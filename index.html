About our company

Da Gama's food truck was founded in class. In fact, our founder was working on a presentation on the Europeans' arrival in India, and at the same time in another class they were doing managment exercises on a food truck. That is where he got his idea from. At Da Gama's food truck, it is not about reproducing typical Indian dishes, but rather exploiting what Indian culture has tought Europeans and creating better food thanks to this knowledge. Our food is not Indian, it is Indian inspired !

The menu

Our menu changes every week, some of our dishes are typical revisited Indian dishes, but most of our cuisine is remastered European cuisine, using other meats and spices, new vegetables to change the 'boeuf bourguignon' into something else, something better we hope :)

The chef

Our chef does not have Indian roots. We chose her because she knows how to surprise our customers by creating new dishes and new combinations, but also because she knows exactly what Da Gama's food truck is all about: reinventing food !
